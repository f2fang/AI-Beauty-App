# **AI Beauty App**

## **Product Vision（产品愿景）**

### **Mission（使命）**

Analyze → Recommend → Track → Improve

发现问题 → 提供方案 → 跟踪变化 → 验证效果

### **Vision（愿景）**

Help users discover skin concerns, receive personalized recommendations, track progress over time, and verify whether skincare products, aesthetic treatments, and lifestyle changes are actually working.

帮助用户发现皮肤问题，提供个性化建议，长期跟踪皮肤变化，并验证护肤品、医美项目和生活习惯是否真正有效。
